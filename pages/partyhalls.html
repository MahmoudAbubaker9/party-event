Party Event

From a lavish wedding reception to an intimate dinner party, we offer a truly unique and central venue

Our talented events team are experts when it comes to creative thinking, having arranged everything from Royal banquets to intimate cocktail parties. We offer the same attention to detail, no matter how large or small your party, and can arrange every event seamlessly, adding sophisticated audio-visual technology and transforming the atmosphere with linens and flower arrangements. Once you have booked your event with us, we will assign you a dedicated Events Manager who will oversee all the details.
